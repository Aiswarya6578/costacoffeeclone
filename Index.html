Our Story

It began in 1971 when two brothers, Sergio and
Bruno Costa, moved to London with the desire to
make great coffee. They went on to blind-test 112
different variations of coffee before arriving at the
perfect one, the ‘Mocha Italia’, which remains our
signature blend to this day.
